# Zerg Units

## Drone

### Pros
* 5 extra hp
* HP regen

### Cons
* Expended to make buildings

## Overlord

### Pros
* Mobile Scout

### Cons
* Slow (until upgrade)

#### Overseer

## Queen

### Pros
* Good anti-air capabilities
* Great support abilities
* Resource efficient, supply efficient

### Cons
* Extremely slow off creep
* Poor ground DPS

## Zergling

### Pros
* Fastest unit in game (on creep)
* Great DPS with adrenal glands
* Incredibly cheap

### Cons
* Fragile

## Baneling

### Pros
* Great AoE damage to light armor

### Cons
* Expensive
* Self-destructive
* Slow off creep

## Roach

### Pros
* Cheap
* Lots of HP

### Cons
* Supply expensive

## Hydralisk

### Pros
* High DPS

### Cons
* Expensive
* Fragile
